About Me

Hi there! My name is Daniel Kramer, and I am a Software Engineer with a Masters student in Astroinformatics with a passion for Big Data.

Throughout my academic career, I have developed a strong interest in the field of Big Data and its potential to revolutionize the way we analyze and understand astronomical phenomena. As part of my studies, I have worked on several research papers in this area, including finding the slowest rotating asteroids, implementing a period finding algorithim on GPUs, and deriving properties for 30,000 asteroids.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also a proud dog owner. My furry friend, Venus, brings a lot of joy and companionship to my life.

When I'm not immersed in my studies or spending time with Venus, I am a passionate sports fan. I root for Atlanta sports teams, and I enjoy following their successes and challenges.

Overall, I am a driven and dedicated individual who is committed to pursuing my academic and personal goals with enthusiasm and determination. Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me, and I look forward to exploring potential opportunities with you.
